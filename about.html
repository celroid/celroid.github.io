Why this website?

   This website has been created as part of a school project.

    I wanted to make a website about a unique, interesting topic and yet still nice to look at. In kaiseki cuisine aesthetics are very important and they are also important for a website, so I think this choice works well to point that out.

    The fact that I've only recently heard about kaiseki cuisine for the first time and it has picked my interest enough to start reading about it has influenced my decision to pick this theme for the website as well .
